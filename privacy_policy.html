Privacy Policy for Dexi

Last Updated: August 11, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Dexi. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it. We keep it simple: Dexi only collects crash and diagnostic information to make the app more reliable. We do not collect names, email addresses, or other contact details.

What We Collect

We use a crash reporting service (Crashlytics) to collect app diagnostics such as:

  • Device model, OS version, and app version
  • Crash logs, stack traces, and performance data
  • Approximate timestamps related to the crash event

No images, card scans, personal identifiers, or contact information are collected.

How We Use This Data

Crash data is used solely to investigate issues, fix bugs, and improve stability and performance.

Sharing & Processing

Crash data is processed by our analytics provider (Crashlytics) acting on our behalf. We do not sell your data. We do not share data with advertisers.

Retention

Crash reports are kept only as long as needed for debugging and in accordance with our provider’s standard retention practices.
